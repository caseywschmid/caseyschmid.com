About the project

I had a blast with this project! We used a premium theme and really took advantage of some of its most interesting capabilities. Some of the main highlights of this project are:

  1. Custom logo design
  2. Implementation of application that allows the vendor to easily provide 24-hour chat support to their customers.
  3. Employment of dynamic shopping image that allows the customer to view a collection of items together in one look and shop those items interactively.
  4. Modern search feature that provides an updated clickable list as the customer types.

Conclusion

This store showcases how a store can utilize a clever design to display a large number of quality items for sale. Making it easy for your potential customers to find what they are looking for with the use of dynamic searches and filters will undoubtedly increase customer conversion rate and average order value.
